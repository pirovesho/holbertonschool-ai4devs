# CarpoolConnect

## Vision
Enable companies to reduce their environmental footprint and commuting costs by providing an intelligent corporate carpooling platform that connects employees with compatible commute partners.

## Users
- **Corporate Employees**: want an affordable and convenient way to commute, reduce fuel expenses, and easily find coworkers with similar routes and schedules for carpooling.
- **HR & Facility Managers**: want tools to support company sustainability initiatives, monitor employee participation and satisfaction, and optimize parking space usage and transportation planning.

## Key Features
- Smart matching algorithm that pairs employees based on commute routes, schedules, and preferences to maximize carpool compatibility.
- Corporate Single Sign-On (SSO) integration for secure access through existing company systems.
- Environmental impact tracking that measures reduced CO₂ emissions from shared commuting.
- Cost savings analytics showing financial benefits for employees and the company.
- Parking optimization tools that help organizations manage parking demand and encourage shared rides.
