# Monolithic vs Microservices Architecture Comparison

| **Aspect** | **Monolith** | **Microservices** | **Winner & Why** |
|------------|--------------|-------------------|------------------|
| **Scalability** | Entire application must scale together | Each service can scale independently | **Microservices**: More flexible and efficient scaling |
| **Deployment** | Single deployment for the whole app | Separate deployments per service | **Monolith**: Simpler for small teams |
| **Complexity** | Simple structure, easier to understand | Distributed system, harder to manage | **Monolith**: Easier for beginners and MVPs |
| **Fault Tolerance** | Failure can affect entire system | Failures isolated to specific services | **Microservices**: Better resilience |
| **Cost** | Lower infrastructure and maintenance cost | Higher due to multiple services and tools | **Monolith**: More cost-effective initially |
