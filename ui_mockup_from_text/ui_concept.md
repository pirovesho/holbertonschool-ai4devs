# UI Concept - Expense Mate

## Purpose
A real-time, collaborative group expense manager designed to visualize social spending, settle debts transparently, and gamify group financial dynamics.

## Users
- **Travelers, Friends & Roommates:** Tracking shared costs for vacations or group dinners. Managing monthly rent, utilities, and household supplies.
- **Event Organizers:** Organizing budgets for weddings, parties, or group gifts.

## Core Screens
- **Collaborative Dashboard:** A "Control Center" featuring a personal standing card (Your Balance), a multi-view graph section (Spending Velocity/Trends), and a share-of-spend breakdown.
- **Advanced Expense Ledger:** A detailed, sortable, and filterable transaction list showing "Who Paid," "Who's Split," and "Item Category."
- **Group Member Hub:** A dedicated space to view the P2P (Peer-to-Peer) relationship matrix—showing exactly who owes whom and by how much.

## Actions
- **Dynamic Logging:** Create expenses with automated split calculations across group members.
- **Insight Switching:** Toggle between different data visualizations (e.g., Daily Velocity vs. Category Pie Charts).
- **Debt Settlement:** Initiate "Settle Debt" workflows to clear balances via external payment links or manual confirmation.
